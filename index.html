Features

Track your time

Start the clock, work on your task and record time spent on it.
With metrics, you can see how much you've been productive during the day and the week.

Organize your tasks

Create projects and add them tasks. Add a title, a description, a due date and other optional fields as needed. Sorts tasks at your convenience.
Use the kanban board and the checklists to help you with your workflow.

Offline

Nothing gets sent to the Internet. No AI whatsoever. No subscription.

In later releases, tools to enable collaboration, or integration with other online tools could be developed, but keeping the possibility of using the app offline in mind.
AI will always be a hard no.
